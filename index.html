Featured Project

Stock Backtest Engine

Quantitative Trading Strategy Framework

A sophisticated backtesting system for evaluating algorithmic trading strategies on synthetic equity data. Implements SMA crossover strategy with realistic market simulation and comprehensive performance analysis.

Key Features

  • Geometric Brownian Motion data generation for 500-day synthetic price series
  • Dual Simple Moving Average (SMA) crossover signal generation
  • Portfolio simulation with transaction-level tracking ($10K initial capital)
  • Performance metrics: Total Return, Max Drawdown, Sharpe Ratio
  • Multi-panel visualization of price data, signals, and cumulative returns
